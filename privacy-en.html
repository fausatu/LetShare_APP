6. Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access (Article 15)

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
How: Email letshare.privacy@gmail.com or use the "Download My Data" feature in settings.

6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
How: Edit your profile directly in settings.

6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Article 17)

You can request deletion of your data.
How: Click "Delete My Account" in settings.

6.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)

You can ask us to temporarily stop processing your data while we verify its accuracy or legality.

6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) and transfer it to another service.
How: Request via letshare.privacy@gmail.com

6.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You can object to processing based on legitimate interest (e.g., analytics, profiling).

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

For processing based on consent (e.g., marketing emails), you can withdraw consent at any time via account settings or unsubscribe links.

6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)

You can file a complaint with the supervisory authority (see Section 12).

7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

7.1 Technical Measures

  • HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Password hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • SQL injection protection: Prepared statements and input validation
  • Session security: Secure, HTTP-only cookies
  • Regular backups: Data backed up daily (encrypted)

7.2 Organizational Measures

  • Access control: Only authorized personnel can access data
  • Activity logging: All administrative actions are logged
  • Security audits: Regular vulnerability assessments

7.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the supervisory authority (CNIL) within 72 hours
  • Notify affected users without undue delay
  • Take immediate corrective action
